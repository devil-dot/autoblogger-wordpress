In addition to digestive health, CVS probiotics have also been linked to immune system support. Our gut plays a crucial role in our immune response, and maintaining a healthy gut flora can enhance our immune function. By introducing beneficial bacteria, probiotics can help strengthen our immune system, making us less susceptible to infections and illnesses.

Furthermore, CVS probiotics have been shown to have a positive impact on mental health. The gut-brain connection is a well-established concept, and research suggests that the balance of gut bacteria can influence our mood and mental well-being.
